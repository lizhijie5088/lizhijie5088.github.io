About Us

Jiangsu Lizhijie Intelligent Machinery Co., Ltd. is located at B5, B7, B9, Special Vehicle Industrial Park, Fengxian, Xuzhou, Jiangsu, China.

We are a professional manufacturer of remote control mowers, skid steer loaders, mini crawler dumpers, mini excavator, road roller, integrating R&D, production, sales and export service. OEM & ODM are supported.

  • Customization: color, logo, attachments, package
